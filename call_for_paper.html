CALL FOR PAPERS

Prospective authors are invited to submit full and original research papers not exceeding 12 pages in the template given (Download Template). Kindly register and upload your paper through easychair.

To submit your papers click here - EASYCHAIR.

Peer reviewed accepted and presented papers in the conference will be published in Scopus indexed Springer Proceedings.

“Springer reserves the right to exclude a submission from the proceedings, if the submission does not meet their standards for scope and/or quality.” Publication Ethics

The paper should not be submitted / published in any other Conferences / Journals. The papers should be submitted in electronic format in the areas below but not limited to the given tracks
